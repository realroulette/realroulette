How to play?

We offer a standard European Roulette with a lower edge(<2.70%) as a result of our unique bonus system

A bonus of 10-90% of the total wagered amount of each bet will be paid when the outcome is 0 and this bonus is tiered according to your average wager size.

To place a bet or increase a bet, simply click on a number with the left mouse button.

Click with the right mouse button to decrease or remove the bet

The default bet size can be adjusted to your desired amount by changing the "Chip Size" input in the middle of the table

Payout follows the standard payout for European Roulette; 35:1 for single number bet, 17:1 for split bets, 8:1 for corner bets, 2:1 for outside dozen bets, and 2:1 for outside low/high, odd, even, red or black bets
